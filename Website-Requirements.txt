Make a website for LawnStars — an online platform for lawn and outdoor home services:

What it is:
LawnStars is a U.S.-based online marketplace and booking platform that connects homeowners with local lawn care and outdoor service professionals. It lets you get instant pricing, schedule services online or via a web app, and manage recurring work easily.
It works by letting homeowners bid for lawncare services.

Core features:
* Two portals, one for business owners/lawncare service providers and the other one for home owners/ customers.
* On the customer side -  Easy booking: Enter your address, see a service, ask price for the service, choose a service date, and choose a bid offer you want to send.
* Wide range of services: Includes lawn mowing, edging, weed control, fertilization, lawn treatments, bush trimming, leaf removal, gutter cleaning, pressure washing, junk removal, and more. Include a search system with filters to look for services. Add a location map as well.
* Quality assurance: Pros are vetted (insurance checked and rated), and services come with a guarantee.
* Both the portals should have profiles. Profile for business owner and a profile for customer. 
On business side

How it works:

Enter your address to get pricing.

Choose the date and type of service you need.

A local professional performs the work.

You can manage and pay through the platform or app.

Business model:
LawnStarter itself doesn’t do the lawn work — it partners with independent lawn care providers, helping them grow their business while giving customers a streamlined way to find and book services.

Where it’s available:
The platform operates in thousands of U.S. cities nationwide.

In short, LawnStarter is like an “Uber-style” service for lawn care and other outdoor home maintenance, offering convenience for homeowners and job leads for local lawn care professionals.
